### Playback Controls

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Play/Pause" icon="circle-play">
    Allows users to start or stop the playback of audio content with a single click, providing control over their listening experience.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Skip Forward/Backward" icon="circle-play">
    Users can skip forward or backward through the content, typically by a set interval (e.g., 30 seconds), allowing them to quickly navigate to different parts of the audio.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Speed Control" icon="circle-play">
    Adjust playback speed to listen faster or slower than the normal speed, catering to user preferences and improving accessibility.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Sleep Timer" icon="circle-play">
    Set a timer to automatically stop playback after a certain period, useful for listening before sleep without worrying about the content playing all night.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Bookmarking" icon="circle-play">
    Mark specific points in the content to easily return to them later, enhancing navigation and the overall listening experience.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Chapter Navigation" icon="circle-play">
    Navigate directly to different chapters or sections of the content, ideal for audiobooks and structured podcasts.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Repeat and Shuffle" icon="circle-play">
    Enable repeating of a single track or shuffling through a playlist, adding variety or focusing on specific content as desired.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Volume Control" icon="circle-play">
    Adjust the volume level directly within the player, providing flexibility to match the listening environment.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Crossfade and Gapless Playback" icon="circle-play">
    Smooth transitions between tracks with crossfade or ensure uninterrupted playback with gapless playback, enhancing the listening experience.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Equalizer Settings" icon="circle-play">
    Customize audio output using built-in equalizer settings, allowing users to adjust frequencies to suit their audio preferences.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Lyrics Display" icon="circle-play">
    Show synchronized lyrics for audiobooks with embedded lyrics, allowing users to follow along with the text while listening.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
