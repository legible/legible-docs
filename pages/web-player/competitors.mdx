## Web Player Features

### Audible

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Playback Controls" icon="circle-play">
    - Play/Pause
    - Skip forward/backward (usually 30 seconds)
    - Speed control (adjust playback speed)
    - Sleep timer
    - Bookmarking
    - Chapter navigation
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Content Discovery and Management" icon="magnifying-glass">
    - Personalized recommendations
    - Browse and search for audiobooks
    - Curated collections and categories
    - User reviews and ratings
    - Download for offline listening
    - Sync across devices
    - Wishlist and favorites
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="User Interface and Experience" icon="eye">
    - Detailed metadata (author, narrator, length)
    - Cover art display
    - Dark mode
    - Progress tracking
    - Cloud storage of purchased content
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Account and Settings" icon="gear">
    - Manage library
    - Purchase history
    - Subscription management
    - Notifications for new releases and promotions
    - Customizable app settings (e.g., playback preferences)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Spotify

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Playback Controls" icon="circle-play">
    - Play/Pause
    - Skip forward/backward
    - Repeat and shuffle
    - Volume control
    - Crossfade and gapless playback
    - Equalizer settings
    - Lyrics display (for supported tracks)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Content Discovery and Management" icon="magnifying-glass">
    - Personalized playlists (e.g., Discover Weekly, Daily Mix)
    - Browse and search for music and podcasts
    - Curated playlists and radio stations
    - User-created playlists
    - Collaborative playlists
    - Podcast subscriptions and management
    - Download for offline listening
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="User Interface and Experience" icon="eye">
    - Album art and artist info display
    - Dark mode
    - Follow artists and friends
    - Social sharing and integration (e.g., sharing to social media, Spotify Codes)
    - Connect to external devices (e.g., Bluetooth speakers, Chromecast, Spotify Connect)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Account and Settings" icon="gear">
    - Manage library (songs, albums, playlists, podcasts)
    - Subscription management
    - Notifications for new releases and updates
    - Customizable app settings (e.g., audio quality, download preferences)
    - Data saver mode
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Netflix

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Playback Controls" icon="circle-play">
    - Play/Pause
    - Skip forward/backward (10 seconds)
    - Volume control
    - Subtitle and audio track selection
    - Speed control (adjust playback speed)
    - Next episode auto-play
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Content Discovery and Management" icon="magnifying-glass">
    - Personalized recommendations
    - Browse and search for movies and TV shows
    - Curated categories and genres
    - User reviews and ratings (thumbs up/thumbs down)
    - Continue watching list
    - My List (custom watchlist)
    - Download for offline viewing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="User Interface and Experience" icon="eye">
    - Detailed metadata (cast, crew, synopsis)
    - Cover art and trailer previews
    - Dark mode
    - Episode and season navigation
    - Watch history
    - Profiles for different users
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Account and Settings" icon="gear">
    - Manage profiles
    - Subscription and billing management
    - Notifications for new releases and updates
    - Customizable app settings (e.g., playback preferences, data usage settings)
    - Parental controls and content restrictions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Fable

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Social / Book Club Features" icon="users">
    - Book Clubs: Join or create book clubs to discuss books with friends, family, or public groups.
    - Discussions: In-app discussion forums for each book, enabling users to share thoughts and insights.
    - Reading Guides: Access curated reading guides to enhance the reading experience and facilitate discussion.
    - Annotations and Highlights: Share annotations and highlights within the book club, allowing for a deeper communal reading experience.
    - Progress Tracking: Share reading progress with book club members to keep everyone on the same page.
    - Group Messaging: Group chat functionality to discuss books and organize book club activities.
    - Invitations: Invite friends to join book clubs or reading discussions via email or social media.
    - Notifications: Alerts for new comments, discussions, and book club activities.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Content Discovery and Management" icon="magnifying-glass">
    - Personalized recommendations based on reading history and book club activities.
    - Browse and search for books by genre, author, or popularity.
    - Curated book lists and recommendations from book club leaders and influencers.
    - Download for offline reading.
    - Sync across devices.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="User Interface and Experience" icon="eye">
    - Detailed book metadata (author, synopsis, reviews).
    - Cover art display.
    - Dark mode.
    - Progress tracking.
    - User profiles with reading stats and book lists.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Account and Settings" icon="gear">
    - Manage library and book clubs.
    - Subscription management.
    - Notifications for book club activities and new book releases.
    - Customizable app settings (e.g., reading preferences, notification settings).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
